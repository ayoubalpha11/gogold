What detection depth do I need?

Shallow (8-10") for surface finds, medium (12-15") for most prospecting, deep (20"+) for serious nugget hunting in mineralized soil.

How deep can metal detectors detect?

Detection depth depends on soil conditions, target size, and detector type. Professional PI detectors can reach 20+ inches, while VLF detectors typically reach 12-15 inches.
