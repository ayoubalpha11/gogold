Our Approach

We evaluate metal detectors based on:

  • Quality: Durability, build quality, and reliability
  • Performance: Detection depth, accuracy, and sensitivity
  • Ease of Use: User-friendly controls and intuitive operation
  • Value: Price-to-performance ratio and overall value for money
  • Customer Feedback: Real user reviews and ratings
  • Suitability: How well the detector matches specific needs and experience levels
